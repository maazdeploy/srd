How each check actually works

SRD R370

Fully digital — ID number and registered phone number on srd.sassa.gov.za, or WhatsApp/USSD/phone alternatives. See SRD Status Check for the dedicated walkthrough.

Other grants

Usually confirmed in person at a SASSA local office, or by phoning SASSA's general enquiries line with your ID number and case reference.

Payment order each month

SASSA typically pays grants in stages: Older Person's grants first, then Disability, followed by Child Support and other grants, while SRD R370 is processed in batches later in the month. Exact dates shift slightly each month and are published on SASSA's own payment schedule.

FAQ

Do all grants use the same check process?

Not exactly — SRD is checked mainly online or by phone, while other grants are usually confirmed at a local office or via the enquiries line.

Which grants get paid first?

Older Person's grants generally lead the monthly cycle, with SRD R370 typically processed last.

Can I get more than one grant?

Usually only one grant per person, though a caregiver can separately receive a child support grant for a child in their care.

Check your specific grant

For SRD R370, use the official online portal. For other grants, your nearest SASSA office or the enquiries line can confirm your status.
